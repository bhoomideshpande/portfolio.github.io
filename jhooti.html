Design and Analysis of Algorithms

This course serves as a cornerstone of computer science and computational problem-solving, focusing on the development of efficient and reliable solutions for complex problems. Mastering algorithms is essential for excelling in fields like software engineering, data science, and artificial intelligence.

Key Algorithmic Paradigms Covered:

  • Divide and Conquer
  • Greedy Algorithms
  • Dynamic Programming
  • Backtracking
  • Graph Algorithms

Applications in the Real World:

  • Search and Optimization: Powering search engines, logistics planning, and supply chain management.
  • Graph Algorithms: Enabling navigation tools such as Google Maps.
  • Dynamic Programming: Solving challenges in resource allocation, financial modeling, and bioinformatics.
  • Greedy Algorithms: Addressing scheduling problems, route optimization, and content streaming services.

This course equips students with the theoretical knowledge and practical skills to design cutting-edge solutions across diverse domains.
